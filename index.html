Key Features

SOS

With just one tap, it sends an automated SMS containing your real-time location and a distress message.

Real Time Image Capturing

Instantly take a photo using your device's camera and immediately process or use that image within the app without delay.

Geofencing

Defines a virtual perimeter around a location. When you enter or exit, it triggers notifications, alerts, or automated tasks for enhanced safety.

Local Volunteering

Nearby, verified individuals are alerted to assist in emergencies, enabling faster, community-driven responses before official help arrives.

Shake Detection

Simply shake your phone to activate an emergency alert, notifying contacts and sharing your real-time location instantly.
